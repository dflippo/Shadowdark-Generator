# Ensign Pip

#npc #albion-dominion #servant #witness

**Role:** Young Ensign, Serving Staff  
**Location:** Governor's Mansion  
**Status:** Alive

## Description

A nervous young ensign assigned to serving duties during Colonel Huzzard's
formal dinner. Witnessed suspicious activity at the serving station.

## Appearance

- Young and nervous
- Junior enlisted uniform
- Anxious demeanor

## Personality

- Nervous and eager to please
- Observant but intimidated
- Defensive about his innocence
- Helpful when questioned properly

## Key Events

**Session 07 - Poisoning Investigation:**

Guarding the serving station after the poisoning attempt. Provided crucial
testimony:

> "I didn't do it! I just carried the tray! But... I did see someone loitering
> here before the speech. Someone high ranking. They said they were 'inspecting
> the crystal for flaws'."

## Testimony Details

**What he heard:**

- Heavy boots
- Distinctive sound: "Click-drag. Click-drag."
- One foot dragging slightly

**What he saw:**

- High-ranking officer (couldn't see face clearly)
- Poor lighting at the time
- Officer claimed to be inspecting crystal

## Evidence Contribution

His testimony about the "click-drag" footsteps was crucial - it matched
[[Captain Blackwood]]'s prosthetic toe, helping identify him as the poisoner.

## Key Relationships

- **The Party:** Provided witness testimony
- **[[Captain Blackwood]]:** Unknowingly witnessed the poisoning preparation
- **[[Colonel Huzzard]]:** Serves under his command

## Notes

Represents the low-ranking soldiers who see everything but are often overlooked.
His attention to detail (remembering the boot sounds) proved invaluable to the
investigation.
