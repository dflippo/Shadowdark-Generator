# Alistair Johnson

#npc #jungle-spirits #ghost #deceased #cartographer

**Role:** Deceased Cartographer, Former Owner of Mivvin's Rest  
**Location:** Hidden chambers beneath study (Session 01)  
**Status:** Ghost laid to rest in [[Session 01 - The House on Mivvin's Rest]]

## Description

[[Lady Lara Croft Johnson]]'s eccentric uncle who inherited and built the Mivvin's Rest estate. A brilliant cartographer and astronomer who disappeared years ago while researching "ancient, dark temples and a coming solar eclipse."

**Purpose in Campaign:** Provides historical context and clues about ancient sites through his research and maps.

## Appearance (Ghost)

- Translucent, sad apparition
- Cannot speak
- Wears cartographer's clothing
- Peaceful expression when his work is completed

## In Life

- Eccentric cartographer and astronomer
- Built secret chambers beneath his study
- Created the Font of Healing
- Researched ancient temples and astronomical events
- Mapped the Black River region

## As a Ghost

**Combat Stats:**

[Ghost](../../../shadowdark-library/monsters/manual-monsters/ghost.md) - AC 13, HP 27, ATK 2 death touch +5 (1d8 + life drain) or 1 possess, MV near (fly), S -2, D +3, C +0, I +0, W +0, Ch +4, AL C, LV 6

**Special Abilities:**
- Greater Undead: Immune to morale checks, only damaged by silver/magic
- Incorporeal: Can become corporeal or incorporeal
- Life Drain: 1d4 CON damage, death if reduced to 0 CON
- Possess: Contested CHA check, controls body for 2d4 rounds

## Haunting

Haunted his workshop, attempting to guide visitors to complete his unfinished star chart. Would only become hostile if his work was damaged.

## Puzzle

Required placing wooden star-shaped pieces correctly onto the chart to complete local constellation mappings. When completed, unlocked the western door and laid his spirit to rest with a peaceful smile.

## Legacy

**Left Behind:**
- The Font of Healing (hidden sanctum)
- Complete map of the Black River region
- Research journal on dark temples and solar eclipse
- 150 gp and three emeralds (120 gp each)
- One Potion of Healing
- Secret chamber defenses and traps

## Key Locations

1. **Antechamber:** Pressure plate trap with portcullises
2. **Cartographer's Workshop:** His haunting ground, star chart puzzle
3. **Hidden Sanctum:** The Font of Healing

## Resolution

The party solved his celestial puzzle, completing his life's work. His ghost faded away peacefully, finally able to rest. His discoveries enabled [[Lady Lara Croft Johnson]] to found the League and begin exploration of the jungle.

## Quotes (Journal)

> "Incredible discoveries await in the deep jungle. The temples align with celestial events. The coming eclipse may reveal secrets lost for millennia."

## GM Notes

His research into the solar eclipse and dark temples foreshadows the greater plot involving Zuchotl and the celestial alignments. His maps are essential to the campaign.
