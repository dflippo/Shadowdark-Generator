**Role:** Young dwarf laborer  
**Location:** Mivvin's Rest (Servants' Quarters)  
**Status:** Saved by party in [[Session 03 - Teeth in the Night]]

## Description

A young, wide-eyed, and eager dwarf who dreamed of becoming an adventurer. Star-struck by the "heroes" of the League.

## Appearance

- Young dwarf (early twenties)
- Gap-toothed smile
- Soot-stained from labor work
- Now carries Grimm Ironfoot's heavy pickaxe

## Personality

- **Before the attack:** Eager, hopeful, admiring
- **After Grimm's death:** Hardened, disappointed, bitter toward Doctor Googoo
- Dreams of seeing the Iron Hills mountains again

## Key Relationships

- **[[Grimm Ironfoot]]:** Foreman and father figure (deceased)
- **[[Doctor Googoo]]:** Initially admired, now blames him for Grimm's death
- **The Party:** Owes life-debt but complicated feelings

## Notable Events

- **Session 03:**
  - Gave [[Doctor Googoo]] his lucky stone (river stone with hole)
  - Abducted by giant jaguar in the night
  - Rescued by the party (or killed, depending on outcome)
  - Witnessed Grimm Ironfoot's death in the cult attack
  - Swore life-debt to the party
  - Organized minstrels to mock Doctor Googoo

## Items

- **Lucky Stone:** Smooth grey river stone with natural hole (given to Doctor Googoo, should be returned)
- **Grimm's Pickaxe:** Heavy mining pickaxe, now his

## Character Development

If saved, Durgan becomes a grim, vengeance-focused ally who seeks to prove himself. He blames Doctor Googoo for not being there when Grimm died and has organized mockery through the minstrels.

## Quotes

> "I want to be an adventurer one day. Not just digging ditches and hauling stone."

> "You're not a joke, are you? You're a hero. Show him he's wrong. Show them all."

> "They say he died screaming for help. Trapped under a burning beam. I kept thinking... 'Where are the heroes? Where's the magic?'"
