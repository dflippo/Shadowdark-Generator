# [[Martha]]

#npc #mivvins-rest #cook #staff

**Role:** Head Cook  
**Location:** Mivvin's Rest (Field Kitchen)  
**Status:** Alive

## Description

A large, usually jovial woman who runs the field kitchen at Mivvin's Rest. Provides meals for the staff and laborers.

## Appearance

- Large build
- Wields a metal ladle (and cleaver)
- Practical kitchen attire

## Personality

- **Normally:** Jovial and welcoming
- **Toward cowards/shirkers:** Harsh and judgmental
- Values hard work and courage
- Protective of the workers

## Key Relationships

- **The Workers:** Feeds and cares for them
- **Doctor Googoo:** Contemptuous; refuses to serve him after his absence during the attacks

## Notable Events

- **Session 05:** Refused to serve Doctor Googoo coffee, deliberately pouring it into the dirt
- Banned Doctor Googoo from the kitchen
- Forces him to sneak in to steal food for the basilisks

## Combat

If threatened, would likely fight with kitchen implements (cleaver, meat tenderizer, rolling pin)

## Quotes

> "Coffee is for the workers. For the people building the walls to keep us safe. Not for... not for guests."

> "Looks like we're out. Shame." (while pouring coffee into the dirt)

## Notes

She represents the judgment and resentment of the common folk toward those who don't pull their weight or abandon them in danger.
