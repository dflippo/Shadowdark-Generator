# Lieutenant Sterling

#npc #albion-dominion #military #officer

**Role:** Young Officer, 4th Arbalesters  
**Location:** Governor's Mansion, Mivvin's Rest  
**Status:** Alive

## Description

Young, eager, and desperate for glory. Thinks the party are romantic heroes and
greatly admires Colonel Huzzard.

## Appearance

- Young (early twenties)
- Immaculate uniform
- Eager expression
- Holds sherry glass at social events

## Personality

- Enthusiastic and idealistic
- Admires Colonel Huzzard
- Views the party as romantic adventurers
- Inexperienced but brave
- Sometimes freezes in panic during crises

## Key Relationships

- **[[Colonel Huzzard]]:** Idol and commanding officer
- **The Party:** Admires them as heroes
- **[[Captain Blackwood]]:** Initially trusted, helped subdue him
- **[[Major "Mad Jack" Ponsonby]]:** Intimidated by the gruff veteran

## Notable Events

- **Session 06:** Attended formal dinner, enthusiastically engaged with party
- **Session 07:**
  - Panicked when Huzzard was poisoned
  - Initially blocked party from taking Huzzard to basement
  - Helped subdue Captain Blackwood during arrest
  - Rallied troops after Maied's defeat

## Notes

Represents the naive optimism of young officers who haven't yet seen the true
horrors of war. His character arc involves gradual disillusionment with colonial
ideals.
