## Active Factions

### [[League of Extraordinary Gentlemen]]

**Type:** Adventure Society  
**Status:** Active, Allied  
**Base:** [[Mivvin's Rest]]  
**Leader:** [[Lady Lara Croft Johnson]]  
**First Appearance:** [[Session 01 - The House on Mivvin's Rest]]

#faction #allies #league-of-extraordinary-gentlemen #active

#### Members
- [[Lady Lara Croft Johnson]] - Patron, Leader
- [[Allan Quatermain]] - Hunter, Mentor
- [[Grimm Ironfoot]] - Dwarf Warrior (party member)
- [[Martha]] - Cook, Herbalist
- Other party members (except [[Doctor Googoo]])

#### Goals & Activities
- Recruit exceptional adventurers through trials like [[The Proving Ground]]
- Protect [[Mivvin's Rest]] from jungle threats
- Oppose colonial exploitation (tension with [[Albion Dominion]])
- Respect the balance of [[The Green Hell]]

#### Key Events
- **Session 01:** Introduced, hired party to solve [[Alistair Johnson]]'s puzzle
- **Session 02:** Tested party in [[The Proving Ground]] (only [[Doctor Googoo]] failed)
- **Session 03:** Defended [[Mivvin's Rest]] against basilisk raid
- **Session 06-07:** Diplomatic negotiations with [[Albion Dominion]]

### [[Albion Dominion]]

**Type:** Colonial Military Government  
**Status:** Active, Neutral (Political Tension)  
**Base:** [[Governor's Clearing]]  
**Commander:** [[Colonel Percival Huzzard]]  
**First Appearance:** [[Session 06 - The Albion Dominion]]

#faction #colonial #albion-dominion #military #active

#### Members
- [[Colonel Percival Huzzard]] - Commander, 4th Arbalesters
- [[Lieutenant Sterling]] - Intelligence Officer (replaced [[Captain Blackwood]])
- [[Captain Blackwood]] - Former Intelligence (imprisoned for treason)
- [[Major "Mad Jack" Ponsonby]] - Artillery Commander

#### Goals & Activities
- Establish colonial control over Black River region
- Exploit jungle resources
- Maintain order and law (Albion perspective)
- Suppress native resistance

#### Key Events
- **Session 06:** Arrived with 500 troops, established [[Governor's Clearing]]
- **Session 07:** [[Captain Blackwood]] exposed as traitor, arrested for attempted murder
- **Session 07:** Diplomatic relations strained after [[Maied]] incident

#### Internal Politics
- [[Colonel Huzzard]]: Professional, negotiable
- [[Captain Blackwood]]: Secret agent for The Home Office, working against Colonial Office
- Party aided Dominion against [[Maied]], earning favor

### [[Basilisk Cult]]

**Type:** Monster-Worshipping Cult  
**Status:** **Destroyed**  
**Base:** [[The Serpent's Eye]] (cleared)  
**Leaders:** [[Cult Leader]], [[High Priestess]] (both deceased)  
**First Appearance:** [[Session 02 - The Proving Ground]]

#faction #enemies #basilisk-cult #destroyed #cultists

#### Members (All Defeated)
- [[Cult Leader]] - Head priest (killed Session 05)
- [[High Priestess]] - Second-in-command (killed Session 05)
- Cultists (scattered/killed)
- Basilisk hatchlings (released, now wild)

#### Goals & Activities (Historical)
- Breed basilisks for unknown purpose
- Raid [[Mivvin's Rest]] for sacrifices
- Control [[The Serpent's Eye]] temple
- Kidnap victims like [[Durgan Coalbeard]]

#### Key Events
- **Session 02:** First evidence discovered in jungle
- **Session 03:** Raided [[Mivvin's Rest]] at night, party defended
- **Session 04:** Party infiltrated [[The Serpent's Eye]], rescued [[Durgan Coalbeard]]
- **Session 05:** Party destroyed cult, killed leaders, released basilisk nursery (chaotic comedy ensued)

### [[Jungle Spirits]]

**Type:** Nature Guardian Collective  
**Status:** Hostile (Provoked), Reduced  
**Base:** [[The Green Hell]]  
**Known Members:** [[Maied]] (deceased), [[Alistair Johnson]] (connected)  
**First Appearance:** [[Session 06 - The Albion Dominion]]

#faction #nature-guardians #jungle-spirits #hostile #active

#### Members
- [[Maied]] - Guardian Dryad/Treant (killed Session 07)
- Other unnamed spirits (implied)
- [[Alistair Johnson]] - Former ally? (deceased, unclear connection)

#### Goals & Activities
- Protect [[The Green Hell]] from exploitation
- Oppose [[Albion Dominion]] colonial expansion
- Maintain natural balance
- Punish those who harm the jungle

#### Key Events
- **Session 06:** [[Maied]] first encountered at [[Star Map Temple]], peaceful
- **Session 07:** [[Maied]] poisoned [[Albion Dominion]] soldiers, party tracked and killed her
- **Session 07:** Party may now be viewed as enemies by remaining spirits

#### Future Threat
Unknown - party killed a major guardian, consequences TBD

## Faction Relationships

### Alliances
- **League ↔ Party:** Strong alliance (most party members are League)
- **Party ↔ Dominion:** Functional alliance (aided against [[Maied]])

### Tensions
- **League ↔ Dominion:** Philosophical conflict (preservation vs exploitation)
- **Dominion ↔ Jungle Spirits:** Open conflict
- **Party ↔ Jungle Spirits:** Now hostile (killed [[Maied]])

### Destroyed
- **League ↔ Basilisk Cult:** Defeated (Sessions 04-05)
- **Party ↔ Basilisk Cult:** Destroyed (Sessions 04-05)
