# Allan Quatermain

#npc #mivvins-rest #mentor #league-of-extraordinary-gentlemen

**Role:** Big-game hunter, Explorer, League Adviser  
**Location:** Mivvin's Rest  
**Status:** Alive

## Description

A weathered, famed big-game hunter and explorer who serves as Lady Johnson's adviser. An unparalleled expert on the jungle's mundane dangers, flora, and fauna.

## Appearance

- Weathered and scarred
- Carries a large-caliber heavy crossbow
- Practical explorer's clothing
- Pipe smoker

## Personality

- Gruff and no-nonsense
- Wary of supernatural elements
- Values discipline and competence
- Cynical about "heroes" but respects those who prove themselves
- **Dislikes:** Doctor Googoo (considers him a liability)

## Key Relationships

- **[[Lady Lara Croft Johnson]]:** Employer and friend
- **The Party:** Mentor and evaluator
- **Doctor Googoo:** Openly hostile; seeks to eliminate him
- **[[Durgan Coalbeard]]:** Respected his potential before his death

## Notable Events

- **Session 01:** Welcomed the party to Mivvin's Rest
- **Session 02:** Led the party on "The Proving Ground" walkabout test
- **Session 03:** Arrived late to help fight the giant jaguar
- **Session 04:** Briefed the party on tracking the cultists
- **Session 05:** Assigned Doctor Googoo as "Head Beastmaster" (punishment)

## Combat Stats

Use [Soldier](../../../shadowdark-library/monsters/manual-monsters/soldier.md) or [Knight](../../../shadowdark-library/monsters/manual-monsters/knight.md) stats with heavy crossbow

## Expertise

- Jungle survival
- Tracking
- Beast lore
- Combat tactics
- Speaks multiple local dialects

## Quotes

> "The jungle chews up and spits out men who've faced far worse than a confused old ghost."

> "This is what happens when you don't respect the Green Hell. This is the price of a moment's carelessness."

> "You kept your heads. You learned. You survived. That's all the jungle asks."
