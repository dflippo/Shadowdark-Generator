# Lady Lara Croft Johnson

#npc #mivvins-rest #patron #league-of-extraordinary-gentlemen

**Role:** Patron, League Founder  
**Location:** Mivvin's Rest (Governor's Mansion)  
**Status:** Alive

## Description

Sharp and intelligent, Lady Johnson is a wealthy explorer and adventurer who inherited her uncle Alistair's estate at Mivvin's Rest. She founded the **League of Extraordinary Gentlemen** to fund expeditions into the Black River jungle.

## Appearance

- Well-dressed in colonial-era explorer's attire
- Carries a hand crossbow
- Previously suffered from jungle fever (cured in [[Session 01 - The House on Mivvin's Rest]])

## Personality

- Intelligent and resourceful
- Values bravery and competence
- Can be ruthless when necessary
- Passionate about discovery and artifacts

## Key Relationships

- **[[Allan Quatermain]]:** Trusted adviser and guild member
- **The Party:** Patron and employer
- **Uncle [[Alistair Johnson]]:** Inherited his estate and artifacts
- **[[Colonel Percival Huzzard]]:** Political ally with the Albion Dominion

## Notable Events

- **Session 01:** Cured of jungle fever by the Font of Healing
- **Session 02:** Formally inducted the party into the League
- **Session 03:** Defended Mivvin's Rest during the cult attack
- **Session 06:** Granted official land charter by the Dominion

## Resources

- Owns Mivvin's Rest estate
- Commands household staff and laborers
- Has access to expedition supplies
- Possesses Uncle Alistair's artifact collection

## Notes

Her mansion functions as a museum filled with artifacts from various expeditions. She is willing to fund adventures in exchange for a share of findings.
