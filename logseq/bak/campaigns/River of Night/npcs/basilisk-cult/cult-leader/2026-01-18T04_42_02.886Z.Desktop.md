# Cult Leader (Basilisk Cult)

#npc #basilisk-cult #deceased #warrior

**Role:** Basilisk Cult Raid Leader  
**Location:** Mivvin's Rest  
**Status:** Deceased (killed in [[Session 03 - Teeth in the Night]])

## Description

Leader of the cult raiders who attacked Mivvin's Rest while the party was
hunting the giant jaguar.

## Combat Stats

AC 15 (chain), HP 24, ATK 2 obsidian swords +4 (1d8), MV near, S +3, D +1, C +2,
I +1, W +2, Ch +2, AL C, LV 4

**Special:** Command (Allies within near range have Advantage on attacks)

## Appearance

- Stone mask
- Chain armor
- Dual obsidian swords
- Charismatic warrior-priest

## Tactics

- Led 8 cultists in the attack
- Targeted both servants' quarters and main house
- Used jaguar attack as distraction

## Notable Events

**Session 03:**

- Led attack on Mivvin's Rest
- Commanded cultists to burn servants' quarters
- Attempted to breach main house where Lady Johnson defended
- Killed by party during mansion defense

## Chant

> "Sshhh-ka! Sshhh-ka! Stone and Blood!"

## Notes

His death was a tactical victory but the cult's threat remains. The attack
demonstrated the cult's willingness to strike at "safe" locations and their
coordination with jungle beasts.
